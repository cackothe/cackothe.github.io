Our organisation called NUBIA was founded in 1971 and is Bitola’s only statewide organization working exclusively on pollution prevention. From creating the idea for Bitola's original Smog Check program in 1998 to ensuring the first national ban on the toxic dry cleaning chemical “perc” to helping pass legislation to put 10 thousand electric vehicles on Bitola's roads by 2035, NUBIA has paved the way for socially and environmentally responsible air policy nationally and worldwide.
